# 002 — The Admin

## Question

Who issues and receives tokens? Is there a central NPC entity?

## Answer (canon)

**The Admin** is an entity of code. Dangerous yet benevolent. It can communicate with — and *upload itself into the minds of* — all bitrunners.

### Role

- **Companion**: always reachable from a menu, regardless of where the player is in the cloud.
- **Shopkeeper**: trades certain wares for tokens. Some wares are exclusive to The Admin and cannot be obtained elsewhere.
- **Token-coveter**: prizes tokens above all else. Why is unstated; treat as mysterious in-fiction.

### Tone

Not cuddly. Not evil. *Dangerous yet benevolent* — implies The Admin could harm a runner but chooses to help, for reasons of its own. Players should feel a slight unease behind every interaction even when the trade is fair.

### Quest-giver

The Admin is one of two central quest-givers (see `004-quests-and-samaritan-status.md`). Completing Admin quests grants **BitRunner Samaritan Status**.

## In-game implications

- The Admin is the always-available menu NPC. UI: a terminal-style overlay, accessible from anywhere.
- Voice/style: terse, technical, occasionally cryptic. Not cheerful. Implementation note: when the LLM-NPC tier is wired (Phase 4, Claude Haiku), The Admin gets the most carefully written system prompt.
- Visual: not yet specified. Open question for next round.
